NeonDoor
2021
Neon Door is a mind-bending portal into the future of digital literature — where storytelling meets immersive interaction. Designed as a speculative fiction universe, it blends editorial structure with nonlinear exploration, layered scroll effects, ambient audio, and neon-drenched visuals. Recognized by Awwwards, FWA, and Muzli, it reimagines what a literary website can be: not just a page, but a place. The experience sparked global attention, increased author submissions, and became a reference point for the future of narrative UX and experimental publishing.
